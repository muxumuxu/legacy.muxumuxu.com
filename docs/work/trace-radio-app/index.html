TRACE Radio

  • Client

    TRACE

  • Delivery

    Design, development iOS & Android

TRACE is a French media platform devoted to urban contemporary music. It’s the third most widely streamed French channel globally, and the first in terms of listeners in Africa.

We’ve led the conception of Trace’s new iOS and Android applications, in line with Apple and Android guidelines. The application comprises many territories, each of them with their own radio streams.

TRACE joined the team as partner, so that we could fully understand their business. From day one, we used interactive prototypes.

We delivered these applications with a back-office for desktop and mobile, so that TRACE can autonomously manage, publish and edit territories and radios: anywhere and anytime.
